Privacy Policy

At Silent Library, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your personal information.

This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our library services, both online and in-person.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register for a library card, attend events, or use certain services, we may collect personal details such as your name, address, phone number, email address, and date of birth.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you use our services, including borrowing history, digital resource usage, search queries, and website interactions.
  • Communication Records: We may retain records of your communications with us, including inquiries, feedback, and support requests.

Sharing and Disclosure

We value your privacy and will not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information with third parties if you give us explicit permission.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering library services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the library, its users, or the public.

    • Cookies and Tracking

    Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your online experience. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings, but please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

    Changes to This Policy

    We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on our website with the revised effective date.

    Security

    We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and secure data storage. Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive information, and we regularly review our security practices to ensure your data is protected.

    We Use Your Information

    • Provide and manage your library account.
    • Process your requests for borrowing materials, accessing digital resources, and participating in events.
    • Communicate with you about your account, services, events, and updates.
    • Improve our services and tailor your library experience.
    • Ensure the security and proper use of library resources.

    Rights and Choices

    You have the right to:

    • Access and review the personal information we hold about you.
    • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
    • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal restrictions.
    • Opt-out of receiving non-essential communications.
